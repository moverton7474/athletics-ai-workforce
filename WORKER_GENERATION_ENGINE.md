# WORKER_GENERATION_ENGINE.md

## Purpose
Generate an initial AI team from organization context rather than forcing manual worker creation first.

## Inputs
- organization type/use case
- website URL
- org profile fields
- selected goals
- imported knowledge/context

## Output
A recommended initial team with:
- worker name
- role
- outcome promise
- included integrations
- suggested guidelines defaults
- shared/personal mode recommendation

## Initial Logic
### athletics default team
- Chief of Staff
- Executive Assistant
- Sponsorship Intelligence
- Proposal & Outreach
- Compliance & Coordination

### optional role additions
- Social/Content worker
- Recruiting intelligence worker
- NIL / athlete brand worker
- Reception/front desk worker (later)

## Future direction
- recommendation scoring engine
- industry templates
- usage-based team refinement
- role replacement and backup logic
